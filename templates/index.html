About The Data

The purpose of this visualize car crashes in my local area. As a daily commuter to Washington, D.C., I was curious the number of car crashes because I see at least one once a week

This dataset provides general information about each collision and details of all traffic collisions occurring on county and local roadways within Montgomery County, as collected via the Automated Crash Reporting System (ACRS) of the Maryland State Police, and reported by the Montgomery County Police, Gaithersburg Police, Rockville Police, or the Maryland-National Capital Park Police.\r\n\r\n Please note that these collision reports are based on preliminary information supplied to the Police Department by the reporting parties. Therefore, the collision data available on this web page may reflect:\r\n \r\n-Information not yet verified by further investigation\r\n-Information that may include verified and unverified collision data\r\n-Preliminary collision classifications may be changed at a later date based upon further investigation\r.
